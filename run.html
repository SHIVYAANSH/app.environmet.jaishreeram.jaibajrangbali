💧 What You Can Do – Save Water, Sustain Life

🚿 Take shorter showers
Reduce your shower time by 2 minutes to save water daily.
🚱 Turn off taps
While brushing or washing hands — saves litres every day.
🛠 Fix leaking taps
A single drip per second wastes over 11,000 litres a year.
🌿 Use a bucket, not a hose
Especially while washing vehicles or watering plants.
☁ Harvest rainwater
Collect and use rainwater for cleaning and gardening.
🚽 Use low-flow fixtures
Install water-saving toilets and faucets at home.
🧼 Run full loads only
Don’t waste water on small laundry or dish cycles.
📢 Spread awareness
Educate your friends and family about saving water!
